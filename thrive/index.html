Sustainable in
every way.

Organic

Our clothes are made of natural fabrics; like lyocell, hemp and organic cotton, that are produced without the use of toxic pesticides, fertilizers and GMO’s that are proven to harm the environment, farmers, producers as well as the end consumer.

Fair labor

The workers who made our clothes earn a fair and living wage, in fair and safe workplace conditions.

Toxic free

“Organic” doesn’t always mean toxic free. It might only apply to the cultivation - the garments are likely treated with more or less dangerous chemicals in processing and staining them. T H R I V E, however, guarantees you a non-toxic end product.

Cruelty free

Our store is PETA Vegan approved, assuring you that no animals were harmed in the production of the clothes.

High quality

Our clothes were carefully crafted and are designed to last for many years.

Environmentally friendly

Our clothes are made of organic or recycled garments from manufacturers that take full responsibility for the environment. Fashion is the second largest polluter in the world, but we know it’s possible to make clean clothes!

About the dark side of fashion.

Raw materials

A single conventional cotton t-shirt contains a footprint of one kilogram potentially harmful chemicals, pesticides and fertilizers. About a quarter of the pesticides on earth are released over cotton crops.

Production

In the fashion industry, millions of workers are forced to work under slave labour conditions for minimal wages, and countless animals are being killed or mistreated, in order to bring down the prices so we can buy as much clothes as possible as often as possible.

Waste

Every year millions of garments are discarded as consumers ditch fast-fashion styles for a new wardrobe.

It is estimated that more than 1 million tonnes of textiles are thrown away every year.
